Chef Infra Server is [deprecated](/versions/#deprecated-products-and-versions) and will reach end-of-life status in November 2026.

Chef 360 Platform is the current Chef platform for infrastructure operations and supports multiple operational models, including Declarative State Management (DSM), job-based automation, and compliance workflows.

Contact your Chef account representative to evaluate available options on Chef 360 Platform and determine the most appropriate path based on their existing usage and future requirements.
